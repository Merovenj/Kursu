# Treasury — every coin in daylight

KÜRSÜ is a commons. Those who say it belongs to the people fund it. Money is
treated exactly like votes: written to the same public ledger, verifiable by all.

## Rules

- **Multi-sig only.** No single steward can move funds (e.g. a 3-of-5 Safe).
- **Every movement is logged** to the public ledger with a human-readable memo.
- **What funds are for:** (1) security audit — the one non-negotiable cash line,
  (2) off-shore infrastructure, (3) hardware keys, (4) translation/accessibility.
- **What funds are NOT for:** salaries, office, marketing. Code and labor are
  volunteer. Cash buys only what cannot be contributed in kind.
- **Bounties:** specific tickets may be funded (Gitcoin-style) and disclosed.

## Legal note

KÜRSÜ is structurally independent of any political party. Contributions are NOT
party donations; they fund an open-source commons. Keeping this separation clean
is the first task for legal counsel — see `docs/KVKK.md`.
